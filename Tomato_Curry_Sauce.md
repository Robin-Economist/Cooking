### Instructions
1.  **Preparation:** Chop the garlic finely.
2.  **Mix:** In a bowl, combine the tomato base with a generous drizzle of olive oil.
3.  **Season:** Add the curry, basil, and the secret herb (oregano?).
4.  **Combine:** Mix everything vigorously until the oil is well incorporated.
5.  **Serve:** Great with pasta, rice, or as a dip for chips!
