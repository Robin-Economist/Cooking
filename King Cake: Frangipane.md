### Instructions

1.  **Prepare the Cream:**
    * Cut the butter into small pieces and place them in a mixing bowl (*terrine*).
    * Work the butter with a spatula to soften it completely.
    * Add the sugar and continue mixing until it turns into a creamy texture.
    * Whisk in the beaten eggs (for the cream), the flour, the rum (if using), and the almond powder. Mix until the preparation is smooth and homogeneous.

    * Brush the top with the beaten egg (from the base ingredients) to make it golden.
